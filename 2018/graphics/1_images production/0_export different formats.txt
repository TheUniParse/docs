
★using photoshop|gimp:
  *export different formats: (export/save-for-web ctrl+shift+alt+s)
    •jpeg: (photographics, gradient colors & soft edges images)
      ›quality: 0~100% lossy
      ›progrissivw: -size
      ›blur: 0~2 -size
      ›metadata(copyright, source, comment)
      ›embed color profile
      
    •png: (flate colors & hard edges images)
      ›quality: lossless
      ›interlaced(progressive): +size
      ›metadata(copyright, source, comment)
      ›embed color profile
      ›matte(if transparency disabled matte will remplace it, & edges dither): none(transparent), Eyedropper, Foreground, background, custom color.
      »png-24: (256'multi levels of transparency)
        »transparency: variable(256 level, better opti-method)
      »png-8:
        ›colors table: 2 ~ 256
        »transparency: binary|variable
          ›if disabled: transparency remplaced by 'matte color'
          ›dither: 0~100%
            _dither-method (anti-aliasing: smooth edges & bg contrast by 'matte'): none, diffusion(0~100%), pattern, noise.
      »Apng: (not supported by photoshop|gimp)
        ›animation
        ›??
        
    •gif: (flate colors & hard edges images, animations)
      ›quality: lossless
      ›colors table: 2 ~ 256
      »transparency: binary
        ›dither: 0~100%
          _dither-method (anti-aliasing: smooth edges & bg contrast by 'matte'): none, diffusion(0~100%), pattern, noise.
      ›interlaced(progressive): +size
      ›metadata(copyright, source, comment)
      
    •webp: (better than jpeg, png-24, png-8, Apng, gif)
      »lossy: better than jpeg
      »lissless: better than png-24, png-8, Apng, gif


  *transparency:
    •png-24: save Truecolor-data in 4 channels r, g, b, a
    •png-8: save 256color-data in 1 channel as rgba(,,, 0~1)
      ›not supported by photoshop|gimp, can not generate multi levels of transparency.
    •png-8|gif: save 256color-data in 1 channel as rgba(,,, 0|1)
      »useless for multi-llevel transparency:
        ›issue: by defualt generate halo's(gradient frome edge-colors to white|black) not suited to bg-color.
        ›fix: set matte→none, dither→100%, dither-method→pattern
