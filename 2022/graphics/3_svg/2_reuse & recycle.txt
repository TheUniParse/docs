

*markup:
  <svg id=a>
    <symbol id=drawn viewBox='0 0 150 200'>
      <!--shapes, lines, pathes …-->
    </symbol>
    <use xlink:href=#drawn>
  </svg>
  
  <svg style=width:60vmin;height:80vmin>
    <use xlink:href=#drawn>
  </svg>
  
*description:
  •symbol element store elements & effects for reuse like <defs>, but also have attribute viewBox for scaling.
  •use element can call content of <g>|<defs>|<symbol>
