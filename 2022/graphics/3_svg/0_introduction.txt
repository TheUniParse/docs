  *mather language of:
    •xhtml→html→html5
    •svg
    •MathML: Used to describe mathematical notation
    •RSS(Really Simple Syndication, or RDF Site Summary): allows your content to be shared as data and read with RSS feed readers
    
  *Syntax Requirements: (applay to external.svg)
    •elements & attributes must be lowerCase
    •elements must be enclosed, & <emptyElement />
      »inline-svg require space character before enclosing, just if last attribute value not enclosed by quotations.
        ›ex: 
          _external.svg:
            <rect width='400' height='200'/>
          _inline-scg:
            <rect width='400' height='200'/>
            <!--or-->
            <rect width=400 height=200 />
    •attribute values must be enclosed by single|double quotation with no-extra white spase inside it !!
    •there are no attributes minimizations like: checked, instead use checked='checked'
    •most special charactere must be refernced by thems numeric unicode.
    •Scripts must be contained in a CDATA section so they will be treated as simple text characters and not parsed as XML markup
    •Proper nesting of elements is strictly enforced.



★svg:
