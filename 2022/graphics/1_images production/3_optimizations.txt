

★general optimization strategys:
  •start with original image(high resolution) to opt from it.
  •limite dimensions: crope unnecessary margins
  •reusing cached images if possible
  •use appropriate tools (photoshop|sketch|paintshop pro)
  •use optimizers tools 'gulp-imagemin, gulp-webp'
  
*optimize jpeg's:
  •reduce quality: 50~70% generally, 30~40% depending.
  •blur|smooth unnecessary content expect important content.
    ›and blur all content by 0.2
  •avoid hard-edges(high contrast details), work with soft-edges(gradient contrast, less details)
    ›compression hard-edge = 4 × soft-edges file-size !!!
    ›use png-8 on hard-edges(or webp)

*optimize png-24's: (useless, use png-8|webp)
  •avoid photographs in favore of jpeg's.
  •convert to png-8(with variable transparency)
    ›link: https://tinypng.com/

*optimize png-8|gif:
  •reduce table|map coloes: start by 5-bit(32color), & increase as necessary 6-bit(64color), 7-bit(128color).
    ›image→mode→index-color
  •disable dithering or reduce it ≤ 30%
    ›dithering is binary-gradient between two colors to create third color unavailable in table|map colors in indexed-colors.
    ›usefull for photogeaphics(blended-colors), not flat-colors.
  •edit images at flat-colors 
    ›better than dithering (blended-colors).
